why isolate together


Need of the Hour

We asked many of the Covid affected patients from our friends,family or acquaintances that what was the worst thing about being a Covid patient.And majority of them replied that it was the boredom which made them feel horrible about their quarantine. Just being surrounded by 4 walls and zero human interaction would make anyone go crazy.They were already fed up of the social media sites and wanted something new for entertainment.

Hence keeping in mind these modern world problems, we decided to make "Isolate Together", an application where the patients can come and reduce their boredom by various means.

Our Motive


We would provide services for entertainment of the users like chatting,games and much more.By this,we aim to destress the already stressed lives of the affected patients.


Apart from killing boredom, users can use this amazing platform for many productive things like making contacts with people having similar interests, getting cured rapidly by suggestions of home remedies.This application would basically become a bridge between the patients and the recovered users.


We live in an era where technology is advancing at an unprecedented rate.There are many pros and cons in the same. Thus we would try to be fruitful in every service we provide.Would need your co-operation.Other than that you are always welcomed. :D


See you onboard in this wonderful journey.
